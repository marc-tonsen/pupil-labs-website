The tenth anniversary of the ACM Symposium on Eye Tracking Research & Applications (ETRA 2018) will be held in Europe! The goal of ETRA is to bring together computer scientists, engineers and behavioral scientists in support of a common vision of moving eye tracking research and its application forward.

Join us in Warsaw to celebrate 20 years of eye tracking research at ETRA!
